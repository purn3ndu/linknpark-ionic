Welcome to City Savior

City Savior is a map-based mobile application where you can post any civic issue and get it solved through appropriate channels.

How to use it?

Explore your neighborhood or any other area on the map and see the various issues or ideas that are reported. Upvote the ones you think are important.

Be the top savior of your country.

Earn Karma points for every activity that you do on the app. This score ranks you in the leaderboards of your city, state and country.
