About and Help Facilitating effective solutions for civic issues around you. Explore your neighborhood or any other area on the map and see the various issues or ideas that are reported in those area. If you notice an issue that you agree with or want solved then up vote that issue and also comment on it. If you see any persisting problem in your neighborhood then you can point the location on the map and post the issue there. Other users of the app will be able to see the issue raised by you and upvote and comment on it. Once the issue is verified as genuine, it will get reported to the relevant authorities for solutions. It will help more if you are able to provide pictures of the issue you faced. You can also suggest improvement ideas for public places that you often see. Posting an issue To post an issue, click on the post issue button to enable the marker.
Point the location on the map using the marker and click post button to post issue there.
You can use the camera shortcut to post issue in your current location.

Issue status Each post belongs to one of the five available states. These states are represented by the borders of the markers on the map. 1. Review - When the post is submitted, it is in Review state. The authorities review the post and verify if the issue is genuine or not. It is represented by blue border.
2. Verification - Once the post is verified by the authorities as genuine, it is in Verification state and it is reported to the relevant authorities. It is represented by red border.
3. In-progress - When the relevant authorities work on the post, the status of the post is In-progress. It is represented by yellow border.
4. Closed - Once the issue is resolved, the status is changed to Closed. It is represented by green border.
5. Archived - Once the post is resolved and closed, after a period of 7 days the status is changed to Archived. Archived posts are not shown on the map. But the user can see the posts in My posts. Setting Home Area Home Area is the area on the map for which the user is interested to receive notification. To set home area, enable add/edit notification area option.
After enabling this option, one can move the notification area circle as one moves the map. To resize the radius, drag the side of the circle to increase or decrease the radius.
Once the desired area has been set click the update button to update home area.
